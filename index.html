Basic components of 4 store Bike engine A piston , a connecting rod , crankshaft Piston moves up and down ; this lateral motion gets converted into rotatory motion in crankshaft The longer the stroke of the piston , the larger the displacement of the gas and air occurs in the cylinder To propel that piston , we need to ignite a tiny bit of gas and whole lot of air , this creates expansion inside the cylinder The fuek Delivery System consist of Intake Manifold , intake exhaust valves , a fuel injector and a spark plug A 4 stroke engine is called so because it takes 4 strokes for the piston to complete a cycle . 1st : intake stroke (piston moves down) , 2nd : compression stroke (piston moves up) {the spark plug fires) , 3rd: combustion stroke , 4th : exhaust stroke
